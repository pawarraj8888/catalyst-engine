Catalyst Engine A research workflow that scores upcoming US earnings prints and logs every call publicly
Live —

Catalyst Engine watches 241 US large-cap stocks. Each morning it scores the next two weeks of earnings prints from 0 (no signal) to 10 (very high conviction), then logs every call publicly with a timestamp. After each company reports, the engine checks whether the actual 1-day move was bigger than that stock's usual move — a verifiable hit-or-miss record that builds in the open over time.
